## Takeaways

- The **stairs sliding issue is bad** and needs to be fixed first because it affects basic movement.
- The **theme score is low**, which makes sense since the level is really simple and doesn’t have much identity yet.
- The **overall score is kind of mid (3/5)** which also tracks. The map is very basic right now.
- Difficulty might be **a bit too easy**, so I probably need to push encounters more.
- Ammo might be **slightly off**, since one person said not enough.
- Navigation being **5/5 is good**, so at least players know where to go.
- Pacing and encounters scored pretty high, so even if the level is simple, those parts seem to be working.
- Overall I need to **add more detail, fix movement issues, and make the map feel less bare**.
